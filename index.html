About Us

Amidst the thickly populated concrete jungles of the suburban Kurla, of Mumbai district, DBIT is an oasis of greenery. With a huge football ground and abundant lush green trees it's full of serenity and is a home to a variety of birds. With our Don Bosco values, we the students, faculty & staff of DBIT pledge to make our campus carbon neutral, create environmental awareness and promote sustainability with sustainable lifestyle using innovative engineering.
